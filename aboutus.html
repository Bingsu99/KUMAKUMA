What is an Izakaya?
Izakaya (居酒屋) (Japanese: [izakaja])

The Japanese word Izakaya (居酒屋) is made up of three kanji with the meaning, in order, “stay-drink-place.” A spot to grab a drink, settle in, and get comfortable. Traditionally characterized by its relaxed ambiance, an Izakaya serves as a gathering place where friends and colleagues come together to enjoy a casual yet diverse menu.
